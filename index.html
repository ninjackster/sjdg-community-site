Our Town

Where the highlands
slow everything down

Nestled at 1,980 meters above sea level in the municipality of Tepatitlán, San José de Gracia is a historic town of cobblestone streets, the centuries-old Parroquia de San José, and the kind of unhurried warmth the Altos de Jalisco are known for. Come for a weekend — stay for the cold ones, the tequila, and the homemade chorizo and cheese.

Festividades

Celebrations &
Traditions

San José de Gracia comes alive throughout the year with religious festivals, cultural gatherings, and the warmth of Alteño traditions.

🌸

March 19

Fiesta Patronal de San José

The town's biggest celebration — honoring Saint Joseph, the patron saint. Though the feast day falls on March 19, the festivities are celebrated during the first 10 days of May with masses, processions, music, and fireworks in the plaza.

🕯️

March / April

Semana Santa

Holy Week brings solemn processions and religious observances through the cobblestone streets, a deeply rooted tradition in the Altos.

🇲🇽

September 15–16

Fiestas Patrias

Independence celebrations fill the jardín with music, lights, and the traditional Grito de Independencia at midnight on September 15.

🌹

December 12

Virgen de Guadalupe

One of Mexico's most beloved celebrations — mañanitas, misa de gallo, and pilgrims honoring the Virgin through the night into dawn.

🎄

December 16–24

Las Posadas

Nine nights of candlelit processions, singing, piñatas, and ponche — a cherished Mexican Christmas tradition celebrated neighborhood by neighborhood.

🛖

Every Sunday

Tianguis Semanal

The weekly market brings fresh produce, local cheeses, handmade goods, and antojitos to the town center — a living piece of everyday Alteño culture.

Getting Here

How to get to
San José

San José de Gracia sits in the heart of the Altos de Jalisco highlands. Getting here is part of the experience.

🚗

By Car from Guadalajara

Take Highway 80 (Carretera Guadalajara–Lagos de Moreno) northeast toward Tepatitlán. Continue past Tepatitlán and follow signs for San José de Gracia. ~95 km · ~1h 30 min.

🚌

By Bus

From Central de Autobuses Guadalajara (Nueva Central), take a bus to Tepatitlán (~1h). From there, take a local colectivo or taxi to San José de Gracia (~20 min).

✈️

From GDL Airport

From Aeropuerto Internacional de Guadalajara (GDL), head northeast via Highway 80. The drive is approximately ~110 km · ~1h 45 min. Rental cars are available at the airport.
